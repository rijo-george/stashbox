What's inside

📷 Scan Receipts

Point your camera at any receipt. On-device OCR extracts store, date, amount, and serial number. Your data never leaves your phone.

🔔 Smart Reminders

Get notified 90, 30, 7, and 1 day before a warranty expires. Customize per product. Never miss a claim window.

🛠 Service Records

Log every repair, maintenance, and inspection. Track costs, link to warranties, build a full ownership timeline.

💰 Cost of Ownership

See the real cost of everything you own. Purchase price plus every repair and service. Track savings from warranty claims.

🔎 Search Everything

Find any asset by name, brand, retailer, or category. Filter by status. Everything is one search away.

☁️ iCloud Sync

Data syncs across all your devices via iCloud. No account to create, no server to trust. It just works.

🎨 6 Themes

Dark, Light, Sunset, Ocean, Forest, Rose. Each with warm colors designed for the content you're looking at.

📄 Export Anything

Export individual assets as PDF receipts or all your data as CSV. Your data is always portable.

No account. No cloud service. No tracking. No ads. Just a quiet app that watches the clock on your warranties so you don't have to. Your data never leaves your devices.
